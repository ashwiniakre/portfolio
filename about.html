Who am I?

Hi, I'm Ashwini Akre, a UX designer from Nagpur, India. I've recently completed my internship at Sparklin. I graduated from Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology with a degree in architecture. I developed a passion for design that extended beyond traditional architecture during my studies. I'm excited to share my journey into the world of UX design and would love to chat more about it when we connect.

My background in architecture has given me a strong foundation in problem-solving, attention to detail, and understanding user needs. As an architect, I developed skills in critical thinking and designing functional, aesthetically pleasing spaces that enhance user experience. My transition into UX design allows me to naturally apply these skills in a digital context.

When I'm not designing, you can find me coddling in my other passions, such as painting, traveling, and cooking. I'm a traveler and love meeting new people and capturing painting moments. I'm looking forward to connecting and sharing a passion for design, travel, art, and all things creative!
